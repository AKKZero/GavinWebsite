ByteFight AI: Zero

  • Engineered a competitive Snake AI using sophisticated search algorithms to hunt and eliminate opponents with high efficiency. At the core, the controller uses: BFS and GBFS.
  • BFS: Looks for available space on map and identify confinement opportunities, systematically exploring the board from it's head while avoiding obstacles and enemy positions. The awareness of the snake allow it to be more aggressive than other AI snakes,it analyzes all possible enemy moves and identifies blocking positions using a modified BFS approach that can identify complete blockades, exploit naturally confined positions, and even forces opponents into dead ends through three-step lookahead.
  • GBFS: which uses distance-based heuristics and prioritized state evaluation to determine effective trapping strategies while also accounting for opponent's sacrifice capabilities, and a recursive function that conducts depth-limited searches with accessibility heuristics to best maintain advantageous positions.
